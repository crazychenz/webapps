Guided Hiking Overview

Beartooth Hiking Company (BHC) provides guided hikes through the beautiful Beartooth Wilderness. Currently, BHC provides three hiking opportunities that include:

  • Gardiner Lake
  • Hellroaring Plateau
  • The Beaten Path

If you are interesting in finding out more general information about the Beartooth Wilderness, you may want to try going to Wilderness Connect - Absaroka-Beartooth Wilderness .

Note: BHC is not directly affiliated with Wilderness.net Connect. For more information on Wilderness.net, please see Wilderness.net


Trip Information

All of the hikes provided by BHC are over night hikes. BHC will provide equipment, provisions, and a vetted guide. Each hike has different prices and difficulty levels. Hikes additionally have optional lengths. The cost is calculated on a perday basis for each of the hikes. You can see more specific information in the table below.

Location Duration Difficulty Cost
Gardiner Lake 3 or 5 days Intermediate $40/day
Hellroaring Plateau 2, 3, or 4 days Easy $35/day
The Beaten Path 5 or 7 days Difficult $45/day

All hikes have a 50% surcharge for Sat/Sun hikes.

Difficuly Level Information

  • Easy - An easy hike includes well maintained trails with a very little elevation gain. This difficulty is recommended for young and elderly individuals.
  • Intermediate - An intermediate hike includes well maintained trails with a moderate elevation gain.
  • Difficult - A difficult hike includes rough terrain with increased milages and a significant increate in elevation gain.
